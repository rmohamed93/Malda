## 
  Malda, a being of the cosmos, has been sent to repair the connections between worlds, collectively known as the Tree of Life. There have been reports of 6 different worlds attached to these branches and roots, plunging into chaos as trickster beings have managed to mess with them. As you reach the center of the tree, you find it requires porthole keys to unlock your way to the next. Each key can be found inside the world you enter for each level.


## Description 
  - **Genre:** Fantasy
  - **Play Style:** Puzzle Platformer
  - Malda is a small game, consisting of 6 levels.
     - Levels 1 through 5 will consist of 3 areas:
        - Easy: Checkpoint - temporarily saves the current completion of the level
        - Medium: Checkpoint - temporarily saves the current completion of the level
        - Hard: Checkpoint - temporarily saves the current completion of the level
        - A reward will be received either after the level is completed or at a checkpoint.

## How to Play
### Gameplay Mechanics: 
  The player will parkour across levels with puzzle elements, all while dodging and/or defeating enemies.
  Items found through each level will allow the player to advance further in the game.

### Basic Controls:
  - Movement uses WASD Keys
  - Jump uses the space bar
  - Action/Attack uses the left mouse click


## Features
  - Six fully playable levels
  - Six unlockable items
  - Various enemies
  - Big Bad Evil Guy **(BBEG)** Boss battle on the last level
  - Interactive platforming elements
  - Saving and loading a saved game file
  - Healing
  - Inventory space
  - Inventory management system
  - Checkpoint system

  - Secret areas and easter eggs
